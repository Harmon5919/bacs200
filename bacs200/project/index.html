Welcome

This blog is for those who are looking for a practical introduction to video games. I wanted to share my love for video games in the hopes that others can also find something to love. I want to focus on games that are affordable, available on PC, and not graphics-heavy. I believe there is a game out there for everyone, and you shouldn't have to shell out hundreds of dollars for the latest equipment to play it.
