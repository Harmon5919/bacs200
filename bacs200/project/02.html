Night In The Woods

A Charming Town with a Dark Story

Night in the Woods is an atmospheric side-scrolling adventure game with a dark secret. Follow Mae as she returns to her hometown after dropping out of college. Reunite with old friends and make some new ones.
This game has one of my favorite soundtracks ever. The blend of mesmerizing music with the adorable art style and the deep story gives a great game to play on a relaxing weekend.

Gameplay

The gameplay itself is fairly simple: arrow keys to move left and right and spacebar to jump. The hardest part is a guitar-hero style minigame that requires pressing the right buttons as the notes come across your screen. Overall, though, it is an easy game to play and love.

Where to Buy

Night in the woods can be bought for the Xbox One, Playstation4, and Nintendo Switch. It can also be bought for PC/Mac
on Steam for $15.99 (at the time of posting).
