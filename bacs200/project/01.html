Stardew Valley

If you are reading this, chances are you've heard about the top selling game of 2020: Animal Crossing: New Horizons.

Today is not about Animal Crossing.

    This article is for people who:

  • Do not have a Nintendo Switch
  • Are burnt out from all the Animal Crossing hype
  • Are looking for an alternative to Animal Crossing for any reason

Stardew Valley is not a new game by any stretch. It came out on Steam at the beginning of 2016 and added a multiplayer mode in 2018.
That being said, Stardew valley is an excellent game to pick up if you've got FOMO from seeing endless tweets and instagram posts of perfectly cultivated villages in Animal Crossing.

How does this game stack up?

Is it Scary?
Low
This game is not very scary. There are some monsters that come out at night, which can be mildly frightening.

Learning Curve
Fairly Low
There is a comprehensive tutorial, and the gameplay does a good job of trying to keep you on track.

Commitment Level
Moderate
The commitment level is relatively high, because it takes a while of playing before you start to feel like you're making progress.

Price
Fairly Low
The price at the time of posting (April 17, 2020) is $14.99 (USD).
